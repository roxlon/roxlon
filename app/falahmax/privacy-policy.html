Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application (the App). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send important notifications such as:

  • Ramadan confirmation
  • Eid announcements
  • Important Islamic news and app updates

These notifications are sent as general broadcasts to all users. We do not collect, store, or manage individual user FCM tokens manually.

2. Location Information

The App may request access to your device’s location solely for functional purposes, including:

  • Calculating accurate Athan (prayer) times based on your region
  • Determining Qibla direction

We do not collect, store, track, or share your location data. All location usage happens locally on your device.

3. Online Audio Content

The App allows users to play Surah (Qur’an recitations) streamed from online sources. We do not collect personal data related to audio playback.

4. Prayer Time Sources

Athan and prayer times are obtained from trusted Islamic sources, including acju.lk.

5. Home Screen Widgets & Background Services

The App uses background alarm services to refresh home screen widgets so users can view Athan times without opening the App. No personal data is collected through this feature.

6. Device Features

The App may use certain device features to improve user experience, including:

  • System theme detection (light/dark mode)
  • Vibration for Tasbeeh counter feedback and notifications

These features do not involve collecting or storing personal data.

Advertisements

Currently, the App does not display advertisements. In the future, advertisements may be introduced as a source of income. If ads are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share users’ personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
