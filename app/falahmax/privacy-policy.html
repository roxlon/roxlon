Overview

Welcome to Falah Max, your comprehensive Islamic application for prayer times, Quran, Qibla direction, and more. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our application.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. We believe in minimizing data collection and only accessing what is necessary for app functionality.

Falah Max is designed to enhance your spiritual journey while respecting your privacy. We do not collect personal information for marketing purposes and we do not sell user data to third parties.

Notifications

Falah Max uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send important notifications to users. These include:

  • Eid confirmation announcements
  • Ramadhan commencement notifications
  • Important Islamic news and updates
  • App update announcements

Important Privacy Note

We do not collect individual FCM tokens from users. Notifications are sent as broadcast messages to all users simultaneously. We cannot and do not track which specific users receive or interact with notifications.

Location Data

Falah Max requests location access for specific, limited purposes only:

Purpose How it's used Data Storage
Prayer Times To calculate accurate prayer times for your specific location Not stored or transmitted
Qibla Direction To determine the direction of Kaaba from your current location Processed locally on device

Location Privacy Assurance

We do not collect, store, or transmit your precise location data. Location processing happens locally on your device. The app only uses your location to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction in real-time. Once the calculation is complete, the location data is discarded.

Prayer time calculations are based on data from ACJU (All Ceylon Jamiyyathul Ulama), ensuring accuracy for Sri Lankan Muslims while also supporting international calculation methods.

App Permissions & Features

Audio Playback

The app streams Quran recitations from online sources. We do not control or have access to your listening history through these services.

Home Screen Widget

The widget displays prayer times without opening the app. An alarm service auto-refreshes widget data to ensure accurate timing.

Theme Detection

The app detects your system theme (light/dark mode) to match your device's appearance. This is a local detection only - no theme preference data is collected.

Vibration

Vibration is used for tactile feedback in the Thasbeeh counter and for notifications. Vibration patterns are controlled locally on your device.

Data Collection Summary

Data Type Collected Purpose

Third-Party Services

Falah Max integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For notification delivery (Google)
  • ACJU Prayer Times: Prayer time calculation data source
  • Online Quran Services: For streaming Quran recitations

While we select reputable services, we encourage you to review their privacy policies as they govern data handling by these third parties.

Future Changes

Advertising

To support ongoing development and maintenance, we may introduce advertisements in future versions. Any ads implemented will:

  • Be clearly marked as advertisements
  • Respect Islamic principles and values
  • Not use personal data for targeting without consent

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our app or regulations. We will notify users of significant changes through in-app notifications. Continued use of Falah Max after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
