Child Safety Standards

Falah Max is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, with comprehensive measures to protect children and comply with global safety regulations.

Reporting Concerns

Falah Max allows users to report child safety concerns to contactroxlon@gmail.com. All reports are taken seriously and handled with urgency.

Legal Compliance

The app complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations. We report concerns to regional and national authorities as required.

Support Team

Our dedicated support team is available to address any child safety concerns. We prioritize these issues and respond promptly.

Our Safety Features

  • No User Interaction

    Falah Max does not allow users to interact with each other, eliminating risks associated with social features.

  • No Content Uploads

    Users cannot upload any content to the app, preventing inappropriate material from being shared.

  • No Private Communication

    The app does not include private messaging or any form of communication between users.

  • No User-Generated Content

    Falah Max contains no user-generated content, ensuring all material is curated and safe for all ages.

Report a Safety Concern

If you have any concerns regarding child safety within the Falah Max app, please contact our dedicated support team. We work closely with authorities to ensure all reports are properly addressed.
